2. **Engagement letter + liability/disclaimer** (`03`, `04`) — attorney-reviewed; these are the contract you sign and the thing that caps your risk. *(Blocks transacting safely.)*
3. **Mutual NDA** (`02`) — attorney-reviewed; the counterparty signs before sending code/data.
4. **E&O insurance** (`05`) — bind a Tech E&O + Cyber policy ($1M/$2M to start); lead with the cap + scope + no-investment-advice posture. *(Some clients require proof of coverage; allow lead time — novel service may take iterations to place.)*

Items 1–4 are the gate. With those + a warm intro, you can sign pilot #1.
