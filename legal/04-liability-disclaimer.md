> stated figure and the **specific checks named herein**, computed deterministically from the
> Materials provided by the Client. It is **not** an audit, an assurance engagement, a fairness
> opinion, investment advice, or a recommendation, and it does **not** opine on investment
> merit or certify the absence of overfitting, data leakage, look-ahead bias, survivorship

> date; Calma did not independently source data or confirm the Materials are genuine production
> artifacts. Reliance is limited to the named Client and the stated scope. See full terms in the
> Engagement Letter.

## Long form (incorporated into the Engagement Letter)

**1. Reproducibility-only certification.** Calma's verification re-executes the Client's work
and recomputes the named figure from raw output files. A verdict of **Confirmed** means the
recomputed figure matched the Claim Under Test within the stated tolerance **given the Materials
provided** — nothing more. It is **not** a statement that the underlying strategy, model, or
result is sound, profitable, durable, or free of methodological flaws.

**2. Deterministic verdict.** Every figure and the verdict itself are produced by deterministic
code, not by judgment or negotiation. The same Materials produce the same verdict. No model or
opinion sits in the decision path.

**3. Not investment advice; not an offer.** Nothing in any report is investment, legal,
accounting, or tax advice; a recommendation to buy, sell, hold, allocate, seed, or invest; or
an offer or solicitation. Calma is **not** an investment adviser, broker-dealer, auditor, or
registered public accounting firm, and provides **no assurance under AICPA/PCAOB standards.**

**4. Out-of-scope by default.** Unless a specific extended check is expressly listed in the
engagement, the verification does **not** assess: overfitting; in-sample vs out-of-sample
validity; data leakage or look-ahead; survivorship or selection bias; transaction costs, fees,
or slippage realism; capacity/liquidity; or fraud or fabrication of the underlying Materials.
Absence of a check is not a finding that the issue is absent.

**5. "Can't-confirm" is not a negative finding.** A **Can't-confirm** verdict means the
verification could not be completed to Calma's standard (e.g., non-reproducing code, ambiguous
data binding, uncontrolled randomness). It is **not** a statement that the Claim is false, and
**not** an adverse finding about the Client. The report states the specific unblock required.

**6. Garbage-in limitation.** The verdict reflects only the Materials the Client provided. If
the Materials are incomplete, altered, or fabricated, the verdict reflects only what was run.
A result that reproduces solely because the Client supplied pre-baked or fabricated outputs is
outside the scope of a standard reproducibility verification.

**7. Limited reliance; no third parties.** The report is prepared solely for the named Client,
for the stated scope, as of its date. **No person other than the named Client may rely on it**
without Calma's prior written consent, and the report creates no duty to, and confers no rights
on, any third party.

**8. As-of date.** Findings speak only as of the report date and the Materials' state at that
time. Calma has no obligation to update the report for later changes.

**9. Limitation of liability.** Calma's liability is limited as set out in the Engagement Letter
(capped at fees paid; no indirect, consequential, investment, or trading-loss damages). This
disclaimer is part of the consideration for the engagement and survives its completion.

**10. No warranty.** Except as expressly stated in the Engagement Letter, the verification and
report are provided without warranties of any kind, express or implied, including merchantability
or fitness for a particular purpose.
